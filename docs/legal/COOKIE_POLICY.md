# CONFIT Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026  
**Effective Date:** Upon Platform Launch

## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website or use our app. They help us recognize your device and remember your preferences.

## 2. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

### 2.1 Essential Cookies
**Purpose:** Required for the website to function properly  
**Examples:**
- Session cookies for authentication
- Security cookies (CSRF protection)
- Load balancing cookies

**Duration:** Session to 1 year  
**Can be disabled:** No - these are necessary for basic functionality

### 2.2 Preference Cookies
**Purpose:** Remember your settings and preferences  
**Examples:**
- Language preference (Arabic/English)
- Theme settings
- Size preferences
- Shopping cart contents

**Duration:** Up to 1 year  
**Can be disabled:** Yes, but will affect user experience

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies
**Purpose:** Help us understand how users interact with our platform  
**Examples:**
- Page view tracking
- Feature usage statistics
- Error monitoring
- Performance metrics

**Duration:** 26 months (then anonymized)  
**Can be disabled:** Yes  
**Anonymization:** IP addresses are anonymized

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies
**Purpose:** Deliver relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness  
**Examples:**
- Ad campaign tracking
- Conversion tracking
- Retargeting cookies

**Duration:** Up to 13 months  
**Can be disabled:** Yes  
**Consent required:** Yes - explicit opt-in required

## 4. COOKIE MANAGEMENT

### 4.1 Your Choices
When you first visit CONFIT, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:
- Accept all cookies
- Accept only essential cookies
- Customize your preferences
- Change preferences anytime via "Cookie Settings"

### 4.2 Browser Settings
You can also manage cookies through your browser:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

### 4.3 Opt-Out Links
- Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- Interest-based ads: http://www.youronlinechoices.com

## 5. COOKIE LIFESPAN

| Cookie Category | Maximum Duration | Auto-Delete |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Essential | 1 year | No |
| Preferences | 1 year | No |
| Analytics | 26 months | Yes |
| Marketing | 13 months | Yes |
| Session | When browser closes | Yes |

## 6. LEGAL BASIS

Under Egyptian Law 151/2020 and international standards:
- **Essential cookies:** Legitimate interest (contract necessity)
- **Preference cookies:** Consent (can be withdrawn)
- **Analytics cookies:** Consent (IP anonymized by default)
- **Marketing cookies:** Explicit consent required

## 7. DATA PROTECTION

Cookie data is:
- Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS 1.3)
- Stored securely
- Not sold to third parties
- Anonymized for analytics after 26 months
