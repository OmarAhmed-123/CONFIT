# CONFIT Return & Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026  
**Effective Date:** Upon Platform Launch  
**Policy Version:** 1.0

## 1. OVERVIEW

At CONFIT, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines the conditions under which you can return items and receive refunds.

**Legal Basis:** Egyptian Consumer Protection Law and E-Commerce Regulations

## 2. RETURN ELIGIBILITY

### 2.1 Standard Returns
You may return items within **14 days** of delivery if:
- Item is unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
- Original tags are attached
- Original packaging is intact
- Hygiene seals (if applicable) are unbroken

### 2.2 Non-Returnable Items
The following items **cannot** be returned:
- Undergarments and swimwear (hygiene reasons)
- Custom-made or personalized items
- Items marked "Final Sale"
- Items damaged due to customer misuse
- Digital products (e.g., Style DNA reports)

### 2.3 Virtual Try-On AI Photos
- AI-generated try-on images are not physical products
- No returns applicable for digital try-on services
- Photos are automatically deleted after 7 days per privacy policy

## 3. RETURN PROCESS

### 3.1 Initiating a Return
1. Log into your CONFIT account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select the order and click "Return Item"
4. Choose return reason and preferred resolution
5. Print the return shipping label (free for eligible returns)

### 3.2 Return Shipping
- **CONFIT-covered returns:** Defective items, wrong items sent
- **Customer-covered returns:** Change of mind, size issues, other reasons
- Return shipping costs are deducted from refund if customer-covered

### 3.3 Packaging Requirements
- Use original packaging when possible
- Include all accessories and documentation
- Attach return authorization slip
- Secure package to prevent damage

## 4. REFUND OPTIONS

### 4.1 Refund Methods
| Original Payment Method | Refund Method | Processing Time |
|------------------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Credit/Debit Card | Original card | 5-10 business days |
| Paymob Wallet | Paymob wallet | 3-5 business days |
| Fawry | Bank transfer | 5-7 business days |
| Cash on Delivery | Store credit or bank transfer | 5-7 business days |

### 4.2 Refund Amounts
- **Full refund:** Defective, wrong item, or our error
- **Partial refund:** Item returned with missing accessories or damaged packaging
- **Restocking fee:** 10% for opened beauty products

### 4.3 Store Credit Option
- Receive 110% of refund amount as store credit
- Credit valid for 12 months
- Non-transferable

## 5. EXCHANGE POLICY

### 5.1 Size Exchanges
- Free size exchanges within 14 days
- Subject to availability
- New item shipped after return is received

### 5.2 Color/Style Exchanges
- Allowed within 14 days
- Subject to availability
- Customer pays return shipping

## 6. DEFECTIVE ITEMS

### 6.1 Reporting Defects
Report within 48 hours of delivery:
- Take photos of the defect
- Contact support via in-app chat or email
- Reference your order number

### 6.2 Defect Resolution
We will offer one of:
- Full refund (including shipping)
- Replacement item (free shipping)
- Store credit (120% of value)
- Repair (if applicable)

## 7. DAMAGED IN TRANSIT

If your package arrives damaged:
1. Take photos before opening
2. Document contents
3. Contact us within 24 hours
4. We'll file claim with carrier
5. Full refund or replacement offered

## 8. DONATION PROGRAM RETURNS

Items donated through our platform have special terms:
- Donations are final (no returns)
- Donor receives digital receipt for tax purposes
- Confirmation of receipt from beneficiary
- Items distributed within 30 days of donation

## 9. PROCESSING TIMELINE

| Step | Timeline |
|------|----------|
| Return request | Within 14 days of delivery |
| Return shipping | Customer has 7 days to ship |
| Item received | Within 14 days of shipping |
| Inspection | 1-2 business days |
| Refund issued | 3-5 business days after approval |
| Funds available | Per payment method timeline |

## 10. E-INVOICE REQUIREMENTS

Per Egyptian Tax Authority requirements:
- All returns generate a credit note
- Credit note linked to original invoice
- VAT adjustment calculated at 14%
- Tax records retained for 7 years

## 11. EXCEPTIONS AND SPECIAL CASES

### 11.1 Promotional Items
- Items purchased with coupons: Refund = amount paid (not original price)
- Buy-one-get-one: Both items must be returned
- Bundle pricing: Entire bundle must be returned

### 11.2 Brand Partner Items
Some brand partners have unique policies:
- Displayed on product page
- Overrides standard policy if more restrictive
- Extended return windows available for premium brands

### 11.3 International Orders
Currently not applicable (Egypt-only service).

## 12. CANCELLATIONS

### 12.1 Order Cancellation
- Before processing: Full refund, no fees
- After processing but before shipping: 5% cancellation fee
- After shipping: Follow return process

### 12.2 Subscription Cancellation
- Monthly subscriptions: Cancel anytime
- Annual subscriptions: Prorated refund if cancelled within 30 days
- No refunds after 30 days of annual subscription

## 13. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

If you're unsatisfied with a return decision:
1. Request supervisor review (within 7 days)
2. Consumer Protection Authority complaint
3. Arbitration per Terms of Service

## 14. POLICY UPDATES

We may update this policy:
- Notice posted 14 days before changes
- Orders placed before changes follow old policy
- Major changes notified via email
