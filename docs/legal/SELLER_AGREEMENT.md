## 3. SELLER OBLIGATIONS

### 3.1 Business Requirements
You must maintain:
- Valid Egyptian commercial registration
- Active tax registration (VAT number)
- Business bank account in Egypt
- Product liability insurance (recommended)

### 3.2 Product Standards
All Products must:
- Be authentic and legally sourced
- Comply with Egyptian standards
- Have accurate descriptions and photos
- Be available for immediate fulfillment
- Include all required labeling

### 3.3 Prohibited Items
You may NOT sell:
- Counterfeit or replica items
- Used clothing (except vintage category)
- Hazardous materials
- Items violating intellectual property
- Products requiring special licenses (without approval)

### 3.4 Inventory Management
- Update stock levels in real-time
- Remove out-of-stock items promptly
- Maintain 48-hour fulfillment capability
- Notify Platform of supply disruptions

## 4. PRICING AND PAYMENTS

### 4.1 Product Pricing
You set retail prices subject to:
- Minimum markup requirements (15% above cost)
- Platform price parity guidelines
- Promotional pricing rules

### 4.2 Commission Structure

| Category | Commission Rate | Payment Timeline |
|----------|----------------|------------------|
| Fashion (standard) | 15% | 7 days after delivery |
| Premium Brands | 12% | 7 days after delivery |
| Accessories | 20% | 7 days after delivery |
| Sale Items (>50% off) | 10% | 7 days after delivery |

### 4.3 Additional Fees
- Payment processing: 2.5% (deducted from settlement)
- Return handling: 5% of item value
- Storage (FBN): 0.50 EGP per unit per day after 30 days
- Marketing boost: Optional, pay-per-click

### 4.4 Settlement
- Payments to registered business account
- E-invoice generated for each settlement
- Monthly tax summary provided
- Minimum settlement: 500 EGP (accumulates if below)

## 5. ORDER FULFILLMENT

### 5.1 Fulfillment Options

**Option A: Seller-Fulfilled**
- You handle packaging and shipping
- Must ship within 24 hours
- Provide tracking number
- Use approved carriers only

**Option B: CONFIT Fulfillment (FBN)**
- Store inventory at CONFIT warehouse
- We handle picking, packing, shipping
- Reduced commission rate (12% base)
- Quality inspection included

### 5.2 Shipping Requirements
- Use CONFIT-branded packaging (provided)
- Include packing slip with each order
- Declare accurate item values
- Insure high-value shipments (>2,000 EGP)

### 5.3 Delivery Standards
- Cairo/Giza: 1-2 business days
- Alexandria: 2-3 business days
- Other governorates: 3-5 business days
- International: Not currently available

## 6. RETURNS AND REFUNDS

### 6.1 Return Policy
You must accept returns for:
- Defective items
- Wrong item shipped
- Item not as described

Standard returns (change of mind) optional but recommended.

### 6.2 Return Process
1. Customer initiates return within 14 days
2. Item shipped to CONFIT inspection center
3. Inspection within 48 hours
4. Refund processed to customer
5. Chargeback to seller (if seller fault)

### 6.3 Return Fees
- Customer fault: 10% restocking fee to seller
- Seller fault: Full refund + shipping costs
- Platform fault: No charge to seller
