## 4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

Under Law 151/2020, we process your personal data based on:

1. **Contractual Necessity** (Article 6): Processing necessary to fulfill our contract with you
2. **Legal Obligation** (Article 7): Compliance with tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements
3. **Legitimate Interest** (Article 8): Service improvement, fraud prevention, analytics
4. **Consent** (Article 5): For marketing communications and optional features
5. **Vital Interests** (Article 9): Emergency situations only

## 7. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER LAW 151/2020

Under Egyptian Personal Data Protection Law, you have the right to:

### 7.1 Right of Access (Article 8)
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Article 9)
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 7.3 Right to Erasure (Article 10)
Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability (Article 11)
Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

### 7.5 Right to Object (Article 12)
Object to processing for direct marketing or legitimate interests.

### 7.6 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 13)
Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent at any time without affecting lawful processing before withdrawal.
