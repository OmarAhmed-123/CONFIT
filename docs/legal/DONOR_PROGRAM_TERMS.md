# CONFIT Donor Program Terms and Conditions

**Last Updated:** April 2026  
**Effective Date:** Upon Platform Launch  
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The CONFIT Donor Program connects donors with beneficiaries to redistribute quality clothing items. By participating, you agree to these terms.

**Legal Framework:**
- Egyptian Civil Code (donation contracts)
- Egyptian Tax Law (deductible donations)
- Law 151/2020 (data protection)
- Anti-Money Laundering regulations

## 2. ELIGIBILITY

### 2.1 Donor Requirements
To donate, you must:
- Be at least 18 years old
- Have a verified CONFIT account
- Own the items being donated (legal right to transfer)
- Reside in Egypt (currently)

### 2.2 Ineligible Donors
You may not donate if:
- You are a business donating commercial inventory (see Seller Agreement)
- You have been banned from the platform
- You have violated donor terms previously

## 3. ACCEPTABLE DONATIONS

### 3.1 Eligible Items
- Clean, gently used clothing
- New items with tags
- Accessories (bags, belts, scarves)
- Shoes in good condition
- Traditional wear

### 3.2 Prohibited Items
**DO NOT donate:**
- Undergarments (hygiene reasons)
- Items with stains, tears, or odors
- Counterfeit or fake branded items
- Hazardous materials
- Recalled items
- Items with offensive content
- Political or religious propaganda materials

### 3.3 Quality Standards
All donated items must:
- Be clean and wearable
- Have no defects affecting functionality
- Be honestly represented in photos
- Match the description provided

## 4. DONATION PROCESS

### 4.1 Listing Items
1. Upload clear photos (minimum 3 per item)
2. Provide accurate description (size, condition, brand)
3. Set item as "Available for Donation"
4. Choose pickup or drop-off method

### 4.2 Matching
Our algorithm matches items with verified beneficiaries based on:
- Size requirements
- Geographic proximity
- Preference profiles
- Need priority

### 4.3 Transfer Methods

**Option A: CONFIT Pickup**
- Schedule pickup via app
- Hand item to verified driver
- Receive digital receipt
- CONFIT handles delivery

**Option B: Drop-off Points**
- Deliver to designated location
- Staff confirms receipt
- Receive digital receipt immediately

**Option C: Direct Handover**
- Meet beneficiary at public location
- Both parties confirm transfer in app
- Safety guidelines must be followed

## 5. DONOR OBLIGATIONS

### 5.1 Accurate Representation
- Photos must be recent and accurate
- Descriptions must be truthful
- Defects must be disclosed
- Size measurements must be correct

### 5.2 Timely Transfer
- Items must be available within 48 hours of match
- Pickup appointments must be kept
- No-shows may result in account suspension
- Communication delays must be reported

### 5.3 Communication
- Respond to beneficiary messages within 24 hours
- Be respectful and professional
- Report any issues via official channels
- No off-platform transactions

## 6. BENEFICIARY PROTECTION

### 6.1 Privacy
- Donor sees beneficiary first name only
- No contact information shared
- All communication through platform
- Location shared only at pickup time

### 6.2 Quality Guarantee
Beneficiaries can report issues:
- Item not as described
- Damaged during transit
- Wrong item received
- Unclean items

### 6.3 Resolution
- Investigation within 48 hours
- Donor may be required to replace item
- Repeated violations result in ban
- Beneficiary receives replacement credit

## 7. TAX BENEFITS

### 7.1 Donation Receipts
All donations receive digital receipts with:
- Item description and estimated value
- Date and location of transfer
- Beneficiary organization (if applicable)
- Unique receipt number
- QR code for verification

### 7.2 Tax Deductions
Per Egyptian Tax Law:
- Individual donors: Up to 10% of annual income deductible
- Corporate donors: Up to 20% of net profit deductible
- Receipts valid for 5 years
- Consult your tax advisor for eligibility

### 7.3 Valuation Guidelines
Fair market value for tax purposes:
- New with tags: 70% of retail price
- Excellent condition: 40% of retail price
- Good condition: 25% of retail price
- CONFIT reserves right to adjust valuations

## 8. POINTS AND REWARDS

### 8.1 Donor Points
Earn points for each donation:
- Standard item: 50 points
- Premium brand: 100 points
- New with tags: 150 points
- Bulk donations (5+ items): Bonus 200 points

### 8.2 Point Redemption
Points can be redeemed for:
- Discounts on future purchases
- Free shipping vouchers
- Exclusive access to sales
- Early access to new features
- Partner brand offers

### 8.3 Recognition Program
Top donors receive:
- Digital badges on profile
- "Impact Champion" status
- Annual recognition event invitation
- Social media features (with consent)

## 9. PROHIBITED CONDUCT

Donors must NOT:
- Discriminate based on any protected characteristic
- Solicit beneficiaries for any purpose
- Request payment or favors
- Share personal contact information
- Harass or threaten anyone
- Use donations for commercial purposes
- Donate stolen or illegal items
- Create multiple accounts

Violations result in:
- First offense: Warning
- Second offense: 30-day suspension
- Third offense: Permanent ban
- Serious violations: Immediate ban + legal action

### 10.1 Platform Role
CONFIT acts as a facilitator only:
- We do not own donated items

- We match but do not endorse donors/beneficiaries

### 10.2 Donor Liability
Donors are responsible for:
- Accuracy of item descriptions
- Legal ownership of items
- Items being safe and non-hazardous
- Compliance with these terms

### 10.3 Release
By donating, you release CONFIT from liability for:
- Item quality disputes
- Delivery delays
- Misuse of items by beneficiaries
- Any damages arising from donation
